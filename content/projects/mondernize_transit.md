### The Question
What infrastructure policies should the U.S.adopt to catch up to other developed nations in terms of the availability and reliability of regional and national public transportation, such as high-speed rail?

### Spoiler Alert: The Reccomendation
The  report recommends that the Department of Transportation prioritize the development  of  urban  subway  and  light  rail  where  appropriate,  and  promote transit-oriented design in city planning.

### Alternatives
* Expand Amtrak Routes
* Establish Urban Subway lines, Light Rail or Bus Systems
* Incorporate the concept of Transit Oriented Designs in City Planning
* Adopt Pedestrian-Bicyclist-Friendly Designs

### Criteria
We evaulated each possible alternative using the following criteria
1.  Minimize reliance on highways.
2.  Maximize the use of public transit, local and interstate. 
3.  Establish rail competition with other transportation methods. 
4.  Secure long-term sustainability. 
5.  Maximize interconnectedness within urban regions. 
6.  Ensure robust safety measures for travelers and pedestrians.
